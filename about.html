Who We Are

GLOVIMO Camp (Glory Of Virginity Movement) is a youth revival event in Uganda focused on spiritual growth, positive mindset, and skill-building, emphasizing purpose, hustle, and abstinence ("No sex before marriage") through empowering sessions, activities, and community building, often held around the end of the year. It aims to create a legacy for young people by inspiring them to achieve their dreams, with recent events featuring speakers like Bishop Lwansa Samuel.

Our Mission

"Creating A Legacy" and personal development.

Our Vision

Spiritual revival, mindset transformation, skill development, and positive peer influence.
